Fantasy weapons

Juha Kaikko

A friend of mine asked me to draw some fantasy weapons for his BatMud -themed card game project. I used the descriptions from him and from BatMud as a guide, and I drew them in Blender, and rendered with Blender's cycles renderer.

The sabre 'green death'

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Green_death
Description: "It is a gleaming green blade, a sabre to be more accurate. Its quality is very fine. The hilt, made of the same metal as the blade, but bound with leather for better grip, is embedded with some emeralds."

A Sizzling Katana

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/A_Sizzling_Katana
Description: "This blade radiates hatred. Legend says it was forged by Satan himself for his mightiest lieutenant. Daemonus is an intelligent blade , you can feel its malice run up your arm as you examine its flickering red blade. The grip is wrapped in dragon hide with a moving pattern. "

Sword of wonders

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Sword_of_wonders
Description: "This is the legendary sword of wonders. Some say it is cursed, some say it is blessed, but all that is certain is that it is strange. The blade is five feet long and made of green crystal. The hilt is made of red crystal and the pommel is an octagonal dull grey gemstone.
The pommel - there is something weird about it. "

Mace of Barbarian Lords

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Mace_of_the_Barbarian_Lords
Description: "Wielded by fierce Barbarian Lords, this finely crafted mace has seen many battles. Strangely enough, no nicks or scratches malign the evil beauty of this weapon."

K’prurian, battle mace

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/K'prurian_battle_mace
Description: "This is simply a battle mace, carved from hard, grey wood of the Puric Oak, with mithril inlays and grip of gold wire."

The HUGE (undead) crushing maul

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/The_HUGE_(undead)_crushing_maul
Description: "This maul is most famous and widely known for its' ability to crush undead creatures of the realm."

Hot crystal voulge of DOOM

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Hot_crystal_voulge_of_doom
Description: "It is a simple, unadorned polearm with nine feet shaft and four feet blade made of red crystal. Inside the shaft and blade you can see a faint pulsating red glow. The light appears to pulsate as quickly as your heart! Maybe the weapon is alive! There is some writing on the shaft."

Kraftigar, trident of darkness

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Kraftigar,_trident_of_darkness
Description: "This weapon is not just made to kill. It is made to mutilate, dismember, torture and kill. Its substance seems to absorb light hungrily."

Ruby long spear
(Two different versions to choose from)

http://taikajuoma.ovh/wiki/Ruby_long_spear
Description: "This weapon is a lengthy shaft of enchanted oak, topped with a triangular head of gleaming, translucent ruby. The spear is void of any runes, barbs, or decorations commonly found on enchanted weapons. "

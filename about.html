What should I write... I'm currently unemployed, 39 year old dude living in Hyvinkää, Finland. I've tried all sorts of things for a work, like woodworking, building and assembling outdoor furnitures and playground items, driving a bus and stuff like that. I didn't really like any of that...

My real passion has always been in doing things with a computer, but back when I should have made right decisions about education and stuff, I didn't consider it as a real job. But I've got a vocational degree in business information technology.

So what do I do then? A lot of gaming for starters. But I do some 3d designing too, like models for 3d-printing and fantasy weapons for a friend's card game project. I also have some projects of my own, like a camera 'rotator', or an arduino-controlled motorized panoramic head for a camera. I designed and printed all the plastic parts. The rotator rotates a DSLR camera around it's nodal point and shoots pictures, which can then be stitched into a full photosphere. Rotating the camera around the nodal point minimizes the parallax effect and makes the panorama stitching easier. So I've also done some Arduino programming, that's basically c++. I also know some Java.
